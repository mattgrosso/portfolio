I'm Matt Grosso.

I'm a Front End Developer and Web Designer based in Washington, DC.

I have have always been a curious and adventurous person who has tried to live my life in a responsible and intentional way.

Along the way I've met great people, had many adventures and worn many hats but in 2016 I scratched a long time itch and started a career in web design.

The rest of the site is about my web design work but you can take a look at a few of my other projects here.

In my spare time I design board games.

Dead Last is my most successful game to date.

You can see a review of it here and, if you like the sound of it, you can buy a copy whereever games are sold (like here)

In 2015 some freinds and I established a boardgame convention in Washington, DC called WashingCon.

By 2016 we had 1000 attendees and are looking forward to growing more in 2017.

If you'd like to come this year it will be held on September 9th and 10th. Buy your tickets at washingcon.com
