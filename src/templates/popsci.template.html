Popular Science

Popular Science is a simple blogging platform developed as a group project for The Iron Yard Front End Web Development course.

The project was done as a simulation of a client-developer relationship.

Wireframes and most of the final decisions regarding design were decided by the client (ie that horrible color).

Tech stack

  • Angular
  • jQuery
  • Sass
  • Grunt
  • Mocha
  • Chai
  • Karma
